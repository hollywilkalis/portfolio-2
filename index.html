About me

After several years of jobs where "web content management" was a small part of my duties, I got to spend several month's working full-time with my department's web team, migrating content to a new version of our Sharepoint CMS, and in the process making it mobile responsive and developing training materials for our web editors. I was hooked! Several months and one job change later, a series of happy coincidences offered me the chance to pursue additional technical training, and that's how I landed at Epicodus as a coding student.
